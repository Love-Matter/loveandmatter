need a place to create?

If you're an artist or maker in the Crown Heights or Bedstuy area and need space to cultivate your practice, share your progress with others, or want to create a home for an existing class you already teach.. please connect with us!

You'll find three forms below:

The first is for everyone, we'll be gathering inout on what exactly is the best use of our resources and space given what local artist and makers need.

The second is for anyone who wants to get on the list for early membership and has a vision in mind for what they want to cultivate. As well as questions to give us a better idea of how to structure and price memberships.

The third is for our future artist in residency. Note this is going to take some time to formalize, so this is a very preliminary list that we will use to contact interested artist. A fully application will be required later.

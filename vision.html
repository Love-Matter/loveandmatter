artists need access

For anyone who has lived in NYC for more than a few weeks you know the number one resources is SPACE. It is so limited here, so how can an artist grow their craft and work in a productive way? We need access

Access to tools, techniques, storage.. and most importantly other artists who can help refine and improve our work with feedback and love.

Our goal is to create a space that fosters cross-pollination within the arts. Not just in a theoretical way, but by putting artist from varies forms of exspression in close proximity with each other. What happens when a potter can give feedback to the works of a painter? How impactful would a scuplture's thoughts be to a woodworker creating their next detailed joinery.

By providing space, tools, and resources like classes and commnity run critiques or gallery shows, we look to offer a unique experience for emerging or established creatives in Crown Heights and Bedstuy.
